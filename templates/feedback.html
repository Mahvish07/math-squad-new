Problem #1

Algebra - Beginner

Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 13

Correct!

Great job! Your answer x = 4 is correct.

Not quite right

Your answer "3" is incorrect. Let's see how to solve this problem.

Step-by-Step Solution

Step 1: Understand the equation

We have the equation: 2x + 5 = 13

Step 2: Isolate the variable term

Subtract 5 from both sides:

2x + 5 - 5 = 13 - 5

2x = 8

Step 3: Solve for x

Divide both sides by 2:

2x ÷ 2 = 8 ÷ 2

x = 4

Step 4: Verify your answer

Substitute x = 4 back into the original equation:

2(4) + 5 = 8 + 5 = 13 ✓

Therefore, x = 4 is the correct answer.
